Do you believe in "Everlasting?"

Let me not count love as a fleeting of art,

It stands like a beacon, fixed within the heart,

Everlasting bind its truth to time or ruthless tide,

Unmoved by raging storms where ships collide.

Love alters not when it alteration finds,

Its trueness shines bright in shadowed minds,

Not be bend beneath the heavy weight of passing years;

Everlasting flame that glows despite the night of fears.

Though beauty’s may wither, fade, and die,

Love remnants, as stars in thy boundless sky,

And youths retreat to time’s unyielding hand,

An everlasting thread that nothing can withstand.

If this be spurious, and ever be ever proved untrue,

Then faith and truth are myths, just like the phrase "I love you."
